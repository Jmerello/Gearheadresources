Step 3: Performing an Oil Change

An oil change is one of the most common and essential maintenance tasks. Follow these steps:

  1. Gather Tools and Supplies: Oil filter wrench, drain pan, funnel, new oil, and a new oil filter.
  2. Drain the Old Oil: Remove the drain plug and let the oil drain completely.
  3. Replace the Oil Filter: Remove the old filter, lubricate the new filter's gasket, and install it.
  4. Refill with New Oil: Use a funnel to add the correct type and amount of oil.
  5. Check for Leaks: Start the engine, let it run briefly, then check for any leaks.

Regular oil changes extend the engine's lifespan and improve performance.
