Step 4: Changing Brake Pads

Brake maintenance is critical for safety. Here's how to change brake pads:

  1. Secure the Vehicle: Park on a flat surface, use wheel chocks, and engage the parking brake.
  2. Remove the Wheel: Loosen lug nuts, lift the car, and remove the wheel.
  3. Access the Brake Pads: Unbolt the caliper and carefully remove it (do not let it hang by the brake line).
  4. Replace Pads: Remove old brake pads, compress the caliper piston, and insert new pads.
  5. Reassemble and Test: Reattach the caliper, reinstall the wheel, and test the brakes before driving.

Maintaining brakes ensures your vehicle stops safely and prevents costly repairs.
