Module 1: Safety and Basic Concepts

This curriculum is designed to set you up for success as a first year technician apprentice. Start by clicking on Lesson 1. Complete lessons 1-3 and obtain an 80% or better on test 1 at the end to unlock module 2.

  • Lesson 1: Safety Protocols

    • Introduction to shop safety

    • Shop safety: Personal protective equipment (PPE), fire safety, emergency procedures.

  • Lesson 2: Tool Identification and Handling

    • Introduction to identifying and safely handling automotive tools

    • Overview of hand tools, power tools, and their proper use in the workshop.

  • Lesson 3: Vehicle Systems Overview

    • Introduction to the major systems in vehicles

    • Understanding the engine, transmission, brakes, electrical, and cooling systems.

  • Test 1: Practical Assessment

    • Test to evaluate your understanding of the first module

    • Practical assessment covering lessons 1-3.

    Module 2: Preventative Maintenance

  • Lesson 4: Oil and Fluid Maintenance

    • Introduction to maintaining vehicle oils and fluids

    • Changing oil, replacing fluids, and checking fluid levels.

  • Lesson 5: Filter and Belt Maintenance

    • Introduction to replacing filters and maintaining belts

    • Replacing oil and air filters, inspecting and replacing timing belts.

  • Lesson 6: Tire Maintenance

    • Introduction to maintaining and replacing tires

    • Inspecting tires for damage, rotating tires, and replacing them when needed.

  • Lesson 7: Battery Maintenance

    • Introduction to maintaining vehicle batteries

    • Inspecting, charging, and replacing car batteries.

  • Test 2: Practical Assessment

    • Test to evaluate your understanding of preventative maintenance

    • Practical assessment covering lessons 4-7.

    Module 3: Basic Repairs

  • Lesson 8: Brake System Maintenance

    • Introduction to brake system maintenance

    • Inspecting and replacing brake pads, rotors, and fluid.

  • Lesson 9: Electrical and Lighting

    • Introduction to electrical system maintenance

    • Testing and replacing fuses, bulbs, and electrical connections.

  • Lesson 10: Cooling System Maintenance

    • Introduction to cooling system maintenance

    • Inspecting and replacing radiators, hoses, and coolant levels.

  • Lesson 11: Spark Plug Replacement

    • Introduction to replacing spark plugs

    • Tools and techniques for spark plug inspection and replacement.

  • Test 3: Practical Assessment

    • Test to evaluate your understanding of basic repairs

    • Practical assessment covering lessons 8-11.

    Module 4: Diagnostics

  • Lesson 12: Introduction to Diagnostics

    • Introduction to vehicle diagnostics

    • Using diagnostic tools to identify vehicle issues.

  • Lesson 13: Electrical System Diagnosis

    • Introduction to diagnosing electrical system issues

    • Identifying and fixing electrical faults using diagnostic tools.

  • Lesson 14: Brake System Diagnosis

    • Introduction to diagnosing brake system issues

    • Diagnosing brake system problems and using tools for testing.

  • Lesson 15: Cooling System Diagnosis

    • Introduction to diagnosing cooling system issues

    • Identifying cooling system issues and finding solutions.

  • Test 4: Practical Assessment

    • Test to evaluate your understanding of diagnostics

    • Practical assessment covering lessons 12-15.

    Module 5: Advanced Basics and Comprehensive Review

  • Lesson 16: Tire Balancing and Alignment Basics

    • Introduction to tire balancing and alignment

    • Techniques and tools for balancing and aligning tires.

  • Lesson 17: Engine Troubleshooting

    • Introduction to troubleshooting engine problems

    • Identifying engine performance issues and solutions.

  • Lesson 18: Exhaust System Maintenance

    • Introduction to exhaust system maintenance

    • Inspecting and replacing exhaust system components.

  • Lesson 19: Final Review and Evaluation

    • Final review of all lessons and skills learned

    • Comprehensive review of all modules and skills covered.

  • Test 5: Final Assessment

    • Final test covering all modules

    • Complete assessment of all skills and lessons learned during the apprenticeship.
