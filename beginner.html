Welcome, Future Gearheads!

This curriculum is designed to set you up for success as a beginner technician. Follow the steps below to build your skills.

1. Introduction to Automotive Tools

Learn about essential tools, their functions, and how to use them safely.

  • Wrenches, sockets, ratchets, and other basic hand tools
  • Power tools vs. hand tools
  • Specialty tools

2. Safety and Basic Maintenance

Understanding safety protocols and performing basic maintenance tasks.

  • Workshop safety guidelines
  • Oil changes and tire rotations
  • Brake inspections

3. Understanding Vehicle Systems

Get to know the primary systems in a vehicle and their functions.

  • Engine
  • Fuel system
  • Exhaust system
  • ignition system
  • Electrical system
  • Transmission
  • Suspension and steering
  • Braking system
  • Frame & Body

4. Basic Repairs and Diagnostics

Introduction to common repairs and how to diagnose basic issues.

  • Checking and replacing fluids
  • Reading diagnostic codes (OBD-II)
  • Battery testing and replacement

5. Practical Resources and Tips

Additional resources to support your journey.

  • Charm.li Repair Guides
  • Matco Tools for Beginners
  • Join local automotive forums and communities
