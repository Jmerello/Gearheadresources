Step 2: Basic Safety Procedures

Safety is paramount in any mechanical work environment. Always adhere to these essential safety practices:

  • Wear Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Safety glasses, gloves, and steel-toe boots.
  • Know Emergency Protocols: Familiarize yourself with fire extinguishers, first aid kits, and emergency exits.
  • Proper Lifting Techniques: Lift with your legs, not your back. Use jacks and hoists correctly.
  • Handle Chemicals with Care: Dispose of fluids properly, and avoid skin contact with oil and other automotive chemicals.
  • Keep Work Area Clean: Prevent accidents by keeping tools and parts organized.

Following these procedures reduces the risk of injury and creates a safe working environment.
