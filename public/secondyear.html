Module 1: Advanced Diagnostics

This curriculum builds on your first-year knowledge. Start with Lesson 1 and complete lessons 1-4, then pass Test 1 with 80% or better to unlock Module 2.

  • Lesson 1: OBD-II Systems and Diagnostics

    • Understanding On-Board Diagnostics

    • Using OBD-II scanners to troubleshoot vehicle issues.

  • Lesson 2: Engine Management Systems

    • Advanced engine diagnostics

    • Analyzing engine sensors and control modules.

  • Lesson 3: Advanced Electrical Diagnostics

    • Comprehensive electrical troubleshooting

    • Using multimeters and oscilloscopes for diagnostics.

  • Lesson 4: Fuel System Diagnostics

    • Diagnosing fuel system issues

    • Testing fuel pressure, injectors, and pumps.

  • Test 1: Advanced Diagnostics Assessment

    • Evaluating your understanding of advanced diagnostics

    • Assessment covering lessons 1-4.

    Module 2: Transmission Systems

  • Lesson 5: Transmission Types and Operation

    • Introduction to automatic and manual transmissions

    • Understanding the operation and components of transmissions.

  • Lesson 6: Transmission Maintenance

    • Maintaining and servicing transmissions

    • Fluid changes, inspections, and minor repairs.

  • Lesson 7: Diagnosing Transmission Issues

    • Identifying and addressing common transmission problems

    • Using diagnostic tools and procedures.

  • Test 2: Transmission Systems Assessment

    • Test covering lessons 5-7

    • Practical and theoretical assessment on transmission knowledge.

    Module 3: Suspension and Steering

  • Lesson 8: Steering System Components

    • Understanding steering systems

    • Rack and pinion, power steering, and alignment basics.

  • Lesson 9: Suspension System Maintenance

    • Maintaining suspension systems

    • Inspecting and replacing struts, shocks, and control arms.

  • Lesson 10: Diagnosing Suspension Issues

    • Troubleshooting suspension problems

    • Techniques for diagnosing common issues like noise or poor handling.

  • Test 3: Suspension and Steering Assessment

    • Assessment covering lessons 8-10

    • Practical and written test on suspension and steering systems.

    Module 4: HVAC Systems

  • Lesson 11: Air Conditioning Systems

    • Introduction to automotive HVAC

    • Components and principles of air conditioning systems.

  • Lesson 12: HVAC Diagnostics and Repair

    • Diagnosing and repairing HVAC systems

    • Leak detection, refrigerant recovery, and component replacement.

  • Test 4: HVAC Systems Assessment

    • Assessment covering lessons 11-12

    • Practical and theoretical evaluation of HVAC systems.

    Module 5: Advanced Engine Systems

  • Lesson 13: Engine Overhaul Basics

    • Fundamentals of engine rebuilding

  • Lesson 14: Turbochargers and Superchargers

    • Understanding forced induction systems

    • Operating principles, maintenance, and diagnostics.

  • Lesson 15: Emission Control Systems

    • Emission system overview and compliance

    • Diagnosing and maintaining emission components.

  • Test 5: Advanced Engine Systems Assessment

    • Final test covering lessons 13-15

    • Practical and theoretical evaluation of advanced engine concepts.
